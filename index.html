EXPLORE YOUR WORLD

Welcome to your one-stop resource for a life-changing journey.

Yo! I'm Jeremy Scott Foster.

When I first left home with a one-way ticket to the other side of the world, I didn't have a clue what I was doing (or even, really, where I was going). But that was the point. I didn't want to know. All I knew was that there was so much I didn't know, and I set out on a quest to broaden my perspective as well as my comfort zone.And I did. Experiencing the world changed me, and in recognizing that travel is a powerful platform for personal growth, I made it my personal mission to help others—people like you—recognize just how accessible travel really is. That's why I started this adventure travel blog.Even if you're lacking time or money, there are countless ways to embark on an adventure of your own. And I'm here to guide you through it.
